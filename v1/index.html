01 — About

CRAFT
THAT
MOVES
PEOPLE.

Ali Abbas Jafri is a creative director, producer, designer, and multidisciplinary artist with a sharp eye for bold storytelling and visual innovation.

His creative portfolio includes directing, shooting, and editing multiple high-profile music videos for various leading artists — several of which have garnered millions of views, reflecting both his visual acumen and audience impact.

Beyond production, Ali works collaboratively with artists and their teams to ensure brand alignment across visual content and social media campaigns. Whether on set or in the studio, he is passionate about merging design, culture, and digital media to craft work that moves people and pushes boundaries.
